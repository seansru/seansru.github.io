Intro

Hello! I'm Sean Ru, a Master's CS student at Georgia Tech with a focus on Visual Analytics. I graduated with my Bachelor's in CS at Georgia Tech in May 2024 with a focus on AI/ML and Database & Networking Technologies.

I have a keen interest in leveraging data to drive insights and enhance decision-making through advanced visualization techniques.

I was born in Taipei, Taiwan and moved to the US when I was 4. I grew up in Hacienda Heights, CA and live ~30 minutes from Disneyland!

During my free time, I like to pursue little passion projects (like this website), play video games, scroll aimlessly on Youtube, hang out with friends, and cook/eat food! I am a certified foodie! Like no seriously, I have way too many food photos on my phone. If you were to ask any of my friends who the foodie of the group is, it's me. I also really love cats, thought I don't have one right now, but I will in the future!

Work

I am a student researcher in the Georgia Tech Visualization Lab under Assistant Professor Cindy Xiong Bearfield. My research focuses on visual complexity, with an accepted 2024 IEEE Vis poster on quantifying visual complexity using R statistical analysis on the variation of chart type's average visual complexity ratings. I plan to extend my research to developing a comoprehensive and updated visualization database through the YJVisualization project, inspired by the MASSVIS project. This new project will revise the taxonomy to reflect contemporary design trends and integrating advanced search capabilities to enable efficient clustering and retrieval of similar visualizations.

This past summer, I was a Data Analyst Intern @ Capital One, where I developed a Tableau dashboard that enabled internal teams to filter and analyze over two years of Bankruptcy Complaints data. I also collaborated closely with compliance testers, risk management, business analysts, and software engineers to define SQL and Python scripts, enhance the UI, and streamline the onboarding process for test scripts within the internal testing platform.
